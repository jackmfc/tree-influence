Method & assumptions
AS 2870 Appendix H proximity-ratio approach (after Cameron & Earl): the minimum tree clearance is Dmin = ratio × H, where H is the species' mature height and the ratio is set by site reactivity — 0.5 (slightly reactive / low risk), 0.75 (moderately reactive), 1.0 (highly/very/extremely reactive / high risk) — increased ×1.5 for rows or groups of trees. If the actual distance D is less than Dmin the footing is within the zone of influence and must be designed for the additional reactive movement from tree drying (deeper design suction change / increased differential movement) per AS 2870 Appendix H, or the tree removed/relocated. AS 2870 does not factor tree species/water demand into the ratio.
Planning/clearance screen only. Footing design within the zone of influence requires a site-specific geotechnical assessment. Confirm the site classification, mature tree height and method against AS 2870-2011 Appendix H and the geotechnical report.
